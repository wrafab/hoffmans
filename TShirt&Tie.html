Sublimation Project

Perhaps the project I was looking forward to the most, the T-Shirt was really fun to make. Learning how to photoshop images was interesting. I decdided to make a shirt for the Super Smash Bros. series.

I started by using an image of a standard stadium. Then, I found images of some of my favorite characters from the game.

I superimposed them over the stage image. Finally, I exported it to the T-Shirt programmer and printed it out. And now I have an awesome new T-Shirt.


But with every shirt there must be a tie to match. Even though this is a tee shirt, which is not worn with a tie. I simply repeated what I did with the shirt, only skipping the step of putting it into a shirt specific program.
